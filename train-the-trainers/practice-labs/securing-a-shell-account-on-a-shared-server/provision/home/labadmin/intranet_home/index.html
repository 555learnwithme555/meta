Add your own pages to our intranet

To add your own pages to the intranet, create an intranet_html directory in your home folder. Any files you place there will be served to the intranet. A file named index.html will be your user's intranet home folder.

Once there, visit http://localhost:12345/~your_user_name/ in your Web browser (such as links(1)) to visit your homepage.

A list of all users who have created intranet home pages for themselves can be obtained as follows:

find /home/* -maxdepth 1 -type d -name intranet_html -print 2>/dev/null | cut -d '/' -f 3
